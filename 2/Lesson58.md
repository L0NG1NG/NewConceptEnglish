# A blessing in disguise?

> 复习被动语态
强调句

## 译文

1. 据说弗林利这个小村里有一棵“该诅咒的树”。
2. 就因为报上提到过这棵树，所以现在来弗林利参观的人越来越多。
3. 该树是50年前栽在教堂附近的，但只是近几年才得到了一个坏名声。
4. 据说，谁要是触摸了这棵树，谁就会交上恶运；
5. 如果谁摘了一片树叶，谁就会死去。
6. 很多村民相信此树已经害了不少人。
7. 人们曾请求教区的牧师叫人把树砍掉，但他直到现在也没有同意。
8. 他指出，由于人们从全国各地纷纷前来参观这棵树，它成了一个有用的财源。
9. 尽管有上述种种说法，但游客们还是照常摘树叶和把他们的名字刻在树干上。
10. 然而到目前为止，还没有一个人暴死呢！

## 原文

1. The tiny village of Frinley **is said** to possess a cursed tree.
2. Because the tree **was mentioned** in a newspaper, **the number of** visitors to Frinley **has** now increased.[^1]
3. The tree **was planted** near the church fifty years ago, but **it is only in recent years that it has gained an evil reputation**.[^2]
4. **It is said that** if anyone touches the tree, he will have bad luck；
5. if he picks a leaf, he will die.
6. Many villagers believe that the tree has already claimed **a number of** victims.
7. The vicar **has been asked** to have the tree cut down, but **so far he has refused**.
8. He **has pointed** out **that the tree is a useful source of income**, as tourists **have been coming** from all parts of the country to see it.
9. In spite of all **that has been said**, the tourists **have been picking leaves and cutting** their names on the tree-trunk.
10. So far, not one of them **has been struck** down by sudden death!

### 强调句

🍉形式：It is / was + 被强调部分 + that / who + 其他成分  
举例说明：Tom met Mary in the park yesterday.（汤姆昨天在公园遇见了玛丽。）

| 强调对象 | 强调句式 |
| --- | --- |
| 强调主语 | It was Tom who/that met Mary in the park yesterday. |
| 强调宾语 | It was Mary that Tom met in the park yesterday. |
| 强调地点状语 | It was in the park that Tom met Mary yesterday. |
| 强调时间状语 | It was yesterday that Tom met Mary in the park. |

🍉不是强调句

- It was obvious that he was very embarrassed.(主语从句后置)
- It is said that he has returened from England.(主语从句后置）

[^1]: `the number of` + 可数名词复数，表示“...的数量”,指的是数字本身，在作为主语时，后接单数动词  
`a number of` + 可数名词复数，表示“许多/大量的”,指的是后面的名词，在作为主语时，后接复数动词

[^2]: 强调句
